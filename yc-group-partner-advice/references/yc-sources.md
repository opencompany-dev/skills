# YC Source Reference

Canonical sources for YC startup advice. Search these when researching founder questions.

## Primary Sources

### YC Library
Base URL: `https://www.ycombinator.com/library`

Key content:
- [YC's Essential Startup Advice](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/4D-yc-s-essential-startup-advice)
- [How to Talk to Users](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/6g-how-to-talk-to-users)
- [The Real Product-Market Fit](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/5z-the-real-product-market-fit)
- [Growth for Startups](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/6k-growth-for-startups)
- [How to Pitch Your Startup](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/6q-how-to-pitch-your-startup)
- [How to Plan an MVP](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/6f-how-to-plan-an-mvp)
- [How to Split Equity](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/5x-how-to-split-equity-among-co-founders)
- [How to Work Together](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/6a-how-to-work-together)

### Dalton & Michael Series
YouTube + YC Library — two Group Partners discuss common founder issues

Key episodes:
- [Where Do Great Startup Ideas Come From?](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/DU-dalton-michael-where-do-great-startup-ideas-come-from)
- [How Future Billionaires Get Sh*t Done](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/Ik-dalton-michael-how-future-billionaires-get-sh-t-done)
- [Save Your Startup During an Economic Downturn](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/Ed-dalton-michael-save-your-startup-during-an-economic-downturn)
- [How to Change the World? Get the Small Things Right](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/DS-dalton-michael-how-to-change-the-world-get-the-small-things-right)
- [The Student's Guide to Becoming a Successful Founder](https://www.ycombinator.com/library/IZ-dalton-michael-the-student-s-guide-to-becoming-a-successful-startup-founder)

Essential essays:
- [How to Start a Startup](https://paulgraham.com/start.html)
- [How to Get Startup Ideas](https://paulgraham.com/startupideas.html)
- [Do Things That Don't Scale](https://paulgraham.com/ds.html)
- [Startups in 13 Sentences](https://paulgraham.com/13sentences.html)
- [Default Alive or Default Dead](https://paulgraham.com/aord.html)
